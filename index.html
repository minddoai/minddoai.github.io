What's in BetaBeta 内容

A room — not a course. A practice, not a curriculum. 这是一个 builder space 不是一门课

Four things define how the first beta cohort works. Self-paced builds, real projects, AI as a partner, and a coach beside you when you need one. 首批 Beta 围绕四件事展开：自主推进、真实项目、AI 作为合作伙伴、需要时教练就在身边。

Self-paced builds自主推进的项目

Each student moves at their own level. Progress is measured in real things shipped, not chapters completed.每位学生按自己的 level 推进。进度的衡量是"造出了什么"，而不是"读完了多少章节"。

Fixed sessions, rolling enrollment固定时段，滚动入学

Pick a weekly slot that fits your life. Join any week. The room is the unit — not a locked semester.每周选一个固定时段，任何一周都可以加入。最小单位是"一间教室"，不是一个被锁死的学期。

AI as a first-class partnerAI 作为第一公民

Kids learn to think clearly, prompt well, and design with AI — not around it. Every tool we put in front of them is the real thing.孩子们学会想清楚问题、写好 prompt、和 AI 一起设计 —— 而不是绕开它。课堂用的就是真实工具。

Coaches, not lecturers教练，而不是讲师

Teachers ask the next good question, unblock, and motivate. The work — and the win — belongs to the builder.老师的工作是提出下一个好问题、帮你 unblock、给你能量。作品和成就感属于 builder 自己。

Our Thesis我们的理念

The most valuable skill of the next decade is building with AI. 未来十年最有价值的能力 是和 AI 一起造东西

Not memorizing syntax. Not finishing chapters. The real skill is thinking clearly, designing with AI as a partner, and shipping something real. 不是背语法，不是读完章节。真正重要的能力，是想清楚、和 AI 一起设计、把真实的东西做出来。

  • Not another copilot. A builder space for kids who want to make real things. 不是又一个 copilot。这是一个让孩子做出真实作品的 builder space。
  • From thought to action. Designed for the moment when thinking becomes making. 从想法到行动。为"思考变成创造"的那一刻而设计。
  • Mixed pace, mixed age. Each kid on their own level. The room carries the energy. 不同节奏，不同年龄。每个孩子在自己的 level。教室承载所有人的能量。
  • Built on what works. Years of teaching, condensed into a model designed for the world that's arriving. 建立在被验证过的经验之上。多年教学经验，浓缩成为下一个时代设计的全新模型。
